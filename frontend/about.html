About RuralAsist

Empowering Rural India Through Technology

Our Mission

RuralAsist is dedicated to bridging the digital divide in India by providing rural communities with easy access to government schemes, digital services, and AI-powered assistance.

We believe that every citizen, regardless of their location or technical expertise, deserves access to the benefits and services designed for their welfare.

Our platform simplifies complex government processes and puts essential services at the fingertips of millions of rural Indians.

What We Offer

Government Schemes

Access 50+ central and state government welfare schemes with detailed eligibility information.

Document Scanner

Extract text from documents using advanced OCR technology - works in Hindi and English.

Scam Protection

Report and detect potential scam calls and messages to protect yourself and your community.

AI Assistant

Get instant answers to your questions in Hindi and English with our 24/7 chatbot.
